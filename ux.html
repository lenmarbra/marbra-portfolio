UX Use Case
Magis Customization Tool

Brief

Our mission for this project was to make Magis digital approach more attractive and aligned with their business approach and identity. Magis as a company that have been creating design products for people's homes, offices and public spaces for more than 40 years. It was founded 1976 in Northern Italy and have throughou the years become an eminent global player within the field. High creativity, quality, and excellence are words describing the company well.

Team

Maria Bramer
Franziska Bauer

Mentor:

Andrew Scupelli

Research

In our research we made comprehensive site analysis, competitors’ analysis, SWOT, and gained insights on what areas that were weaker and which ones where good.

Benchmarking

Exploring for features that other similar brands have that might be interesting for Magis.

Problem

As our conclusion based on our research we found that Magis’ site was not aligned with their overall business approach. Magis’ is a brand with high quality and with a strong knowledge within their products, both when it comes to design and functionality. This was not the overall feeling you got when visiting their website.

Solution Proposal

At last we narrowed our focus down to 3 areas:

  • Onboarding (catch the users’ attention)
  • Filter (main feature for he user to navigate and find what they want)
  • Improvements of their Customization page (exclusive feature to offer the users with)
